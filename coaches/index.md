# Coaches

- Samantha McKee
  - Coach Sam is entering her fourth season coaching travel softball and brings knowledge, dedication, and a strong focus on player development. She played college softball at Illinois Central College and Campbellsville University. She is a two-time All Conference/Region player and two-time voted captain by her peers. A former five-year college softball player and catcher, she offers a unique perspective on leadership, game management, and strategy. 
  - She is passionate about helping athletes grow in skill, confidence, and character. Committed to fundamentals and player development, Coach Sam works to create a positive, competitive environment where athletes feel supported, challenged, and empowered to reach their full potential.

- Kelli Dunne
  - Coach Kelli brings a championship-level mindset and an exceptional depth of experience to the program. A four-year, two-sport athlete at Illinois Central College and Eureka College, she went on to coach at the Eureka for seven years within a program that consistently competed at the top of its conference. During her time playing in college, she was a force at the plate. She is still a leader in numerous offensive records at Eureka College, including Home Runs and Batting Average.  
  - Having coached and developed athletes in a highly competitive collegiate environment, she understands exactly what it takes to succeed at the next level from a coaching and player perspective. The daughter of a former MLB baseball player and Olympian, she is passionate about raising the standard, building confident competitors, and helping athletes reach their highest potential both on and off the field.

- Brad McKee
  - Coach Brad brings 18 years of travel softball coaching experience to the program, along with unmatched energy and passion for the game. Over nearly two decades, he has developed athletes at multiple levels while building competitive, disciplined, and team-first environments.
  - Coach Brad  is known for his core philosophy built around the “Three E’s” and his standard that “If you’re walking, you’re running.” His message is simple: bring energy, effort, and execute the game plan. He challenges athletes to move with purpose, compete with intensity, and embrace a fast-paced, high-accountability culture. His experience, consistency, and love for the game help set the tone for a program that values hard work, urgency, and playing the game the right way.
